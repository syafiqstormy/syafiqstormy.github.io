I personally suggest you to buy the Huion H420 Tablet if you are on the budget, Click here to buy H420. nonetheless starting off with a professional tablet does not make you a better artist immediately but it is all up to your personal choice whether to buy a cheap or a high end tablet.

Then,


There are tons and tons of digital painting softwares that are to be found online, most of them are free to use, or by subcription. It really does not matter which painting software that you are using, because everything takes time to master, and if you, a beginner, use a professional tool without knowing anything and diving right away, it would be no less than a child trying to learn. So again I emphasize you can start with even MSpaint or photshop, the outcome will always be the same.
